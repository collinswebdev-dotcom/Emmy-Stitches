About Emmy Stitches

Your Trusted Fashion Designer in Kano

At Emmy Stitches, we don't just sew clothes — we craft identities. With over 8 years of experience in the fashion industry, we have become a trusted name for premium male fashion in Kano and beyond.

From the elegance of agbada to the sharpness of corporate suits, every piece we create is a blend of tradition, modernity, and uncompromising quality. We also design elegant female outfits for special occasions.

Located at No. 32 Emir Road, Sabongari Kano, we are committed to delivering fashion that speaks confidence, culture, and class.
